# Sample Runs

## Regular example
Input:
Enter account number: 1001
Enter service code (r/R for regular, p/P for premium): r
Enter total minutes used: 60

Output:
Account Number: 1001
Service Type: Regular
Total Minutes Used: 60
Amount Due: R120.00

## Premium example
Input:
Enter account number: 2002
Enter service code (r/R for regular, p/P for premium): p
Enter minutes used during the day (6:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m.): 80
Enter minutes used during the night (6:00 p.m. to 6:00 a.m.): 30

Output:
Account Number: 2002
Service Type: Premium
Minutes (day): 80
Minutes (night): 30
Total Minutes Used: 110
Amount Due: R255.00
